# Risk Dashboard Overview  
## Southern Cross Capital Pty Ltd  
### Executive Risk Visualization

---

# 1️⃣ Purpose

This dashboard provides executive-level visibility into cyber risk exposure following AWS cloud migration.

It summarizes:

- Inherent risk distribution  
- Residual risk status  
- Critical risk reduction  
- Control maturity level  

---

# 4️⃣ Residual Risk Profile

Most remaining risks are:

Medium Impact
Medium Likelihood

These require monitoring but do not require emergency remediation.

Residual risks are primarily related to:

- Human phishing exposure  
- Cloud misconfiguration risk  
- Privileged access management

# 6️⃣ Executive Interpretation Guidance

Leadership should interpret the dashboard as:

- Risk is managed, not eliminated.  
- Critical exposures have been addressed.  
- Continuous governance oversight remains necessary.  
- Identity and monitoring remain priority investment areas.  

The dashboard provides decision-support visibility rather than technical detail.
