# NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) Alignment  
## Southern Cross Capital Pty Ltd  
### Cloud Security Governance Mapping

---

# 1️⃣ Purpose

This document maps implemented controls and processes to the NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF).

The NIST CSF is structured around five core functions:

Identify  
Protect  
Detect  
Respond  
Recover  

The objective is to demonstrate structured cybersecurity governance aligned with globally recognised best practices.

---

# 2️⃣ NIST CSF Core Function Alignment

---

# 1️⃣ IDENTIFY

The Identify function focuses on understanding risk, assets, governance, and business environment.

### Your Implementation

- Business context documentation
- Asset identification process
- Data classification model
- Formal risk register
- Likelihood × Impact risk scoring
- Risk heat map
- ISO 27001 control mapping

### Risks Addressed

- Unidentified asset exposure
- Unstructured risk decision-making
- Governance blind spots

### Governance Maturity

Your structured risk lifecycle aligns directly with NIST risk identification and governance expectations.

---

# 2️⃣ PROTECT

The Protect function focuses on safeguards to limit impact of potential incidents.

### Your Implementation

- IAM least privilege enforcement
- MFA for privileged users
- Encryption at rest (S3, EBS)
- Encryption in transit (HTTPS)
- Backup protection
- Patch management process
- Security group hardening

### Risks Addressed

- Credential compromise
- Data exposure
- Ransomware exploitation
- Insider misuse

### Governance Maturity

Preventive controls reduce likelihood and align with financial sector protective requirements.

---

# 3️⃣ DETECT

The Detect function ensures timely discovery of cybersecurity events.

### Your Implementation

- CloudTrail logging
- CloudWatch alerting
- Root login monitoring
- Security group change alerts
- Log retention policy
- Vulnerability scanning simulation

### Risks Addressed

- Undetected breach
- Delayed incident response
- Prolonged attacker dwell time

### Governance Maturity

Detection capabilities reduce incident impact severity and regulatory exposure.

---

# 4️⃣ RESPOND

The Respond function focuses on structured incident management.

### Your Implementation

- Documented incident scenario
- Structured containment procedures
- Eradication process
- Credential revocation steps
- Executive escalation model
- Regulatory impact assessment

### Risks Addressed

- Uncoordinated response
- Regulatory non-compliance
- Extended operational downtime

### Governance Maturity

Demonstrates operational discipline and executive communication capability.

---

# 5️⃣ RECOVER

The Recover function ensures resilience and restoration of services.

### Your Implementation

- Backup restoration procedure
- EC2 rebuild from secure image
- Post-incident monitoring
- Lessons learned documentation
- Risk register updates

### Risks Addressed

- Extended business interruption
- Repeat incidents
- Loss of customer trust

### Governance Maturity

Resilience-focused design aligns with financial sector continuity expectations.

---

- Risk-based security
- Continuous improvement
- Executive oversight

Your implementation demonstrates:

Southern Cross Capital’s cloud security program aligns with NIST CSF by:

- Identifying risks systematically
- Implementing protective safeguards
