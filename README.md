# Enterprise AWS Cloud Risk & Governance Simulation

**Cyber Risk Analyst Portfolio Project**

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project simulates a real-world cyber risk assessment for a mid-sized Australian financial services firm migrating critical systems to AWS cloud infrastructure.

The objective was to identify, assess, and mitigate cloud-related cyber risks while aligning controls with regulatory and governance frameworks such as ISO 27001 and NIST Cybersecurity Framework.

This project demonstrates structured cyber risk lifecycle thinking from asset identification through executive-level reporting.

---

## 🏢 Business Scenario

Simulated Organisation:
**Southern Cross Capital Pty Ltd**

Industry: Financial Services (Australia)

Core Systems Migrated to AWS:

* Online trading platform
* Customer financial records
* Payment processing systems
* Internal administrative systems

Regulatory Context:

* Australian Privacy Act
* APRA CPS 234
* Mandatory data breach notification requirements

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Identify critical business assets
* Perform structured threat modeling
* Develop formal risk register
* Implement security controls in AWS
* Conduct vulnerability assessment
* Simulate real incident response
* Map controls to ISO 27001 & NIST CSF
* Produce executive-level risk reporting

---

## 🧠 Risk Assessment Methodology

Risk Formula:

Risk = Likelihood × Impact

Scoring Scale:
1–5 qualitative model

Risk Categories:

* Low
* Medium
* High
* Critical

Both Inherent Risk and Residual Risk were calculated to demonstrate control effectiveness.

---

## 🔐 Key Security Controls Implemented

* IAM least-privilege access model
* Multi-Factor Authentication enforcement
* S3 encryption at rest
* EBS encryption
* HTTPS-only communication
* CloudTrail logging enabled
* Security group hardening

---

## ⚠️ Top Identified Risks

* IAM privilege escalation
* Public S3 bucket exposure
* Backup integrity failure
* Security group misconfiguration
* Ransomware deployment via compromised credentials

All critical risks were reduced through targeted security controls.
